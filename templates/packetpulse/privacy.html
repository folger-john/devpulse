Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Summary: PacketPulse does not collect, transmit, or share your personal data. Your network traffic data and connection logs stay on your device. We use Google AdMob for ads, which has its own data collection practices.

1. Data We Collect

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Connection logs: Active network connections, remote hosts, ports, and protocol information displayed in real-time. Stored in an on-device database.
  • Traffic statistics: Bandwidth usage, data transfer rates, and historical traffic data.
  • Network info: Local IP address, gateway, DNS servers, and Wi-Fi/cellular connection details.

Data we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect or transmit your network traffic data to any remote server.
  • We do not inspect packet contents or perform deep packet inspection.
  • We do not track your browsing activity or connection destinations.
  • We do not have analytics or telemetry.

2. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements via Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data for ad targeting and measurement. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Security

  • All network monitoring data is processed and stored locally on your device.
  • The app reads network connection metadata from the Android system; it does not intercept or modify network traffic.
  • No data is sent to any external server by the App itself.

4. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App deletes all stored data including traffic history, connection logs, and network statistics.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can clear traffic history or uninstall the App to remove all data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be accessible within the App and at this URL.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@byteoven.com
