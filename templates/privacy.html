Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Summary

DevPulse is a privacy-first platform. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. The data you enter into our tools is processed in-memory and never saved to disk or any database.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not store any input data you submit to our tools
  • We do not use tracking cookies
  • We do not require user accounts or registration
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties
  • We do not fingerprint your browser

Third-Party Services

DevPulse may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — Displays advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com.
  • Google Analytics — Collects anonymized usage statistics (page views, referrers). No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Cloudflare — CDN and DDoS protection. Cloudflare may log IP addresses for security purposes per their own privacy policy.

Data Processing

All tool operations are stateless. When you use a tool (e.g., format JSON, generate a hash), the data is:

  1. Sent to our server via HTTPS
  2. Processed in-memory
  3. Returned to your browser
  4. Immediately discarded — nothing is logged or stored

API Usage

When using the DevPulse API, we may log:

  • IP addresses (for rate limiting only, not stored permanently)
  • Request counts (aggregate, not individual request content)

We never log the content of API requests or responses.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is nothing to request deletion of. If you have concerns, contact us at hello@devpulse.tools.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
