Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Summary: APIBench does not collect, transmit, or share your personal data. Your API requests, collections, and history stay on your device. We use Google AdMob for ads, which has its own data collection practices.

1. Data We Collect

Data stored locally on your device:

  • API requests: URLs, headers, request bodies, and method types you configure. Stored in an on-device database.
  • Collections: Saved groups of API requests organized by project or endpoint.
  • Request history: Previous requests and their responses, stored locally for your reference.
  • Authentication tokens: Any API keys or bearer tokens you enter are stored locally on your device only.

Data we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect or intercept your API request or response data.
  • We do not transmit your API keys, tokens, or credentials to any server other than the ones you specify.
  • We do not track your usage, endpoints, or activity.
  • We do not have analytics or telemetry.

2. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements via Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data for ad targeting and measurement. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Data Security

  • All request data and credentials are stored locally on your device.
  • API requests are sent directly from your device to the target server.
  • The app does not proxy or relay your API traffic through any intermediary.

4. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App deletes all stored data including collections, request history, and saved credentials.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete individual collections, clear history, or uninstall the App to remove all data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be accessible within the App and at this URL.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@byteoven.com
